About Us

Our story and commitment to quality

Our Story

Founded in 1995 by Vinod Agarwal, Agarwal Kirana Store began as a small neighborhood shop with a vision to provide quality grocery items at fair prices. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted wholesale supplier serving businesses across the region.

Our Mission

To be the most reliable wholesale grocery partner for businesses by offering consistent quality, competitive pricing, and exceptional service.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experience: Over 25 years in the grocery business
  • Quality: Rigorous quality checks for all products
  • Relationships: Long-term partnerships with brands and clients
  • Service: Personalized attention to each client's needs
